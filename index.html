Privacy Policy for Hide Calculator

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Summary: Hide Calculator stores all your data encrypted and locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. You have complete control over your data.

Introduction

Hide Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our secure photo and video vault application handles your information.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Hide Calculator stores all data locally on your device. We collect and store:

  • PIN/Password: Your security PIN is encrypted and stored securely on your device using Android Keystore
  • Media Files: Photos and videos you import into the vault are encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption and stored locally
  • App Preferences: Your settings such as theme preferences, auto-lock duration, and other configuration options
  • Albums and Organization Data: Album names, favorites, tags, and organizational information for your media
  • Intruder Detection Photos: If enabled, photos captured during failed unlock attempts are stored locally

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT access your location data
  • We do NOT require account creation or authentication with external services

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All data processed by Hide Calculator remains exclusively on your device. The app:

  • Stores encrypted media files in the app's private storage area
  • Does not upload, sync, or backup data to cloud services
  • Does not require internet connection to function
  • Cannot be accessed by other applications on your device

Encryption

  • Media Encryption: All photos and videos are encrypted using military-grade AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Key Storage: Encryption keys are generated and stored securely using Android Keystore System
  • Device-Bound Security: Encryption keys are bound to your specific device and cannot be extracted

Permissions

Hide Calculator requires the following permissions to function:

Required Permissions

  • CAMERA: To capture photos directly into the vault and for intruder detection feature
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+) or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): To import existing photos and videos from your device into the vault
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): To export media from the vault when you choose to share or move files out

Optional Permissions

  • USE_BIOMETRIC / USE_FINGERPRINT: To enable fingerprint or face unlock for convenient access to your vault

All permissions are used solely for their stated purpose and no data accessed through these permissions is transmitted off your device.

Data Deletion

Secure Deletion

When you delete files from the vault:

  • Files are moved to a recycle bin with a 30-day retention period
  • Permanently deleted files are securely overwritten multiple times to prevent recovery
  • You can manually empty the recycle bin at any time

Uninstalling the App

Important: If you uninstall Hide Calculator:

  • All encrypted data stored by the app is permanently deleted
  • Your PIN, settings, and all vault contents are removed from your device
  • This action is irreversible and data cannot be recovered

Children's Privacy

Hide Calculator does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain content specifically targeted at children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please note that all data remains on the device and can be removed by uninstalling the application.

Third-Party Services

Hide Calculator does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Social media platforms
  • Data brokers

The app operates entirely offline and independently.

Data Retention

  • Vault Data: Retained locally on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • Recycle Bin: Deleted items are retained for 30 days before permanent deletion
  • Settings and Preferences: Retained until you clear app data or uninstall the app

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app interface
  • Modification: You can organize, rename, and modify your vault contents at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual files, albums, or all vault contents
  • Export: You can export media from the vault back to your device gallery
  • Data Portability: Since all data is stored locally, you maintain complete control

Security Measures

We implement robust security measures:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all media files
  • Secure PIN storage with brute-force protection
  • Lockout mechanism after multiple failed attempts
  • Optional biometric authentication
  • Intruder detection with photo capture
  • Secure deletion with file overwriting
  • No network transmission of sensitive data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app or app store listing
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

Legal Compliance

Hide Calculator complies with:

  • Google Play Store policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations

Since we do not collect, store, or process personal data on external servers, most data protection regulations regarding data controllers do not apply to our operations.
